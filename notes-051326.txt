## The Clara voice fix (CRITICAL — required for any Gemini Live app)

Three requirements, must all be met or voice breaks in production:

1. API key declared as `import.meta.env.VITE_API_KEY || process.env.API_KEY` (NOT just `process.env`)
2. `new GoogleGenAI({ apiKey })` with NO `httpOptions` or `api-proxy` — the proxy breaks WebSocket
3. Model string must be `gemini-2.5-flash-native-audio-preview-12-2025`

Per Dan's memory: this is confirmed working on Katie 4/5/2026 and the same fix was applied to ACS during a prior intensive session. **Needs re-verification before demo.**

Why Firebase Hosting and not Cloud Run: Cloud Run's proxy injection breaks the Gemini Live WebSocket. All voice-enabled Gemynd apps must deploy to Firebase Hosting.
