# For the Director (David)

You have the **Director** role — the most complete view of the practice. During
the trial you can see and do everything the Therapist and Office roles can, plus
practice **Settings**. (A few areas are turned off for everyone during the trial;
see the end of this page.)

> To explore the app as your team sees it, sign out and sign back in via
> **"Continue with demo access"** as **Therapist** or **Admin**. It's the fastest
> way to understand each person's experience.

## What's in your menu

- **Dashboard** — your daily snapshot.
- **Clients** — find and open any client record.
- **Calendar** — the appointment schedule.
- **Messages** — the communication center.
- **Forms** — assign forms and review submissions.
- **Treatment Plan Library** — ready-made plan templates to apply to clients.
- **Compliance Risk** — a monitor of client alerts.
- **Financials** — read-only billing reports (daily payments, outstanding
  balances, and revenue). New for the day-30 review; shared with the Office (Admin)
  role.
- **Settings** — practice settings (shown under a **"Reports"** heading). This is
  yours alone; the others don't see it.

## Your dashboard

After signing in you land on the **Dashboard**. Across the top are three quick
figures — **"Compliance Rate," "Monthly Revenue,"** and **"Active Clients"** —
followed by **"Today's Schedule,"** a **"Risk Monitor"** summary
(**Critical / High / Elevated / Moderate**), and a **"Clinical Guardrails"** list
of the top client alerts. With sample data these numbers may read **"—"** or
zero, which is normal.

## Early AI helpers — please try them and tell us

Two AI features are switched **on** for you during the trial. They're early
experiments, not finished tools — the most useful thing you can do is try them
with **made-up information** and tell us what worked.

- **Clara** — the round assistant button in the bottom-right of every screen.
  Ask it a question by typing. See [Finding your way around](02-navigation.md).
- **Clinical Smart Note Studio** — open it from the top **"Schedule"** menu →
  **"Create Note."** It's a space to draft a session note with AI assistance.

> ⚠️ **Sample data only.** Because these features send text to an AI service,
> please don't enter any real client information while testing them during the
> trial.

## Turned off during the trial

These are intentionally hidden for now and will be part of a later setup
conversation: **Analytics** and **AI Documents**. There's a
short, honest summary in [Help & what's next](07-help-and-whats-next.md).

> 💬 **Tell us what you think:** As the person who sees the whole picture, your
> notes on what's missing or out of order are the most valuable feedback we can
> get during these 30 days.
