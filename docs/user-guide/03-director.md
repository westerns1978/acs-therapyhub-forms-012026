# For the Director (David)

You have the **Director** role — the most complete view of the practice. During
the trial you can see and do everything the Therapist and Office roles can, plus
practice **Settings** and the **Compliance Readiness** report. (A few areas are
turned off for everyone during the trial; see the end of this page.)

> To explore the app as your team sees it, sign out and sign back in via
> **"Continue with demo access"** as **Therapist** or **Admin**. It's the fastest
> way to understand each person's experience.

## What's in your menu

- **Dashboard** — your daily snapshot.
- **Clients** — find and open any client record.
- **Calendar** — the appointment schedule.
- **Forms** — assign forms and review submissions.
- **Treatment Plan Library** — ready-made plan templates to apply to clients.
- **Compliance Risk** — a monitor of client alerts.
- **Financials** — read-only billing reports (daily payments, outstanding
  balances, and revenue). Shared with the Office (Admin) role.

Under a **"Reports"** heading at the bottom of the menu, you alone also have:

- **Compliance Readiness** — a practice-wide view of which Missouri compliance
  rules the engine can verify today and which still need data captured.
- **Settings** — practice settings (Google Calendar, Zoom, account details).

## Your dashboard

After signing in you land on the **Dashboard**. It opens with a short plain-English
briefing — a greeting and a sentence summing up today's sessions, open alerts, and
active clients.

Below that you'll see:

- Two summary tiles — **"Open Guardrail Flags"** (how many clients currently trip a
  compliance check) and **"Active Clients."**
- **"Today's Schedule"** — the sessions on the books for today. Click one to open
  its pre-session **Green Room**.
- **"Risk Monitor"** — a count of client alerts grouped as **Critical / High /
  Elevated / Moderate,** with a button to open the full monitor.
- **"Clinical Guardrails"** — the top client compliance flags from the engine, each
  with its plain-language detail and the Missouri rule it relates to. Click one to
  jump to that client.

With sample data these numbers may read **"—"** or zero, which is normal.

## Early AI helpers — please try them and tell us

Two AI features are switched **on** for you during the trial. They're early
experiments, not finished tools — the most useful thing you can do is try them
with **made-up information** and tell us what worked.

- **Clara** — the assistant in the top bar (the small round picture, labeled
  **"Open Clara clinical assistant"**). Ask her a question by typing. See
  [Finding your way around](02-navigation.md).
- **Smart Note Studio** — open it from the top **"Schedule"** menu →
  **"Create Note."** It's a space to draft a session note, with an option to have
  AI tidy your raw text into a SOAP or DAP format before you save it to the record.

> **Sample data only.** Because these features send text to an AI service, please
> don't enter any real client information while testing them during the trial.

## Turned off during the trial

These are intentionally hidden for now and will be part of a later setup
conversation: **Messages**, **Analytics**, and **AI Documents**. There's a short,
honest summary in [Help & what's next](07-help-and-whats-next.md).

> **Tell us what you think:** As the person who sees the whole picture, your notes
> on what's missing or out of order are the most valuable feedback we can get
> during these 30 days.
