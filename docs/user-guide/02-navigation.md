# Finding your way around

Once you sign in, the screen has three constant parts no matter where you go:

- a **menu down the left side** (your main navigation),
- a **bar across the top** (search, quick actions, and your profile), and
- a **round "Clara" button** floating in the bottom-right corner.

> 📱 **On a phone or small screen,** the left menu is tucked away. Tap the
> **menu icon** (top-left) to slide it out. A couple of items live in slightly
> different spots on mobile, but everything below still applies.

---

## The left-hand menu

This is how you move between the main areas of the app. Click any item to open
it. The item you're currently in is highlighted. You won't see *every* item
below — the menu only shows what your role uses (and a few areas are turned off
for the trial; see the bottom of this page).

| Menu item | What it's for | Who sees it during the trial |
|---|---|---|
| **Dashboard** | Your home screen and daily snapshot. | Everyone |
| **Clients** | Find a client and open their record. | Everyone |
| **Calendar** | The appointment schedule (view and add sessions). | Everyone |
| **Messages** | The communication center for client and team messages. | Everyone |
| **Forms** | The library of forms — assign them and review what's sent back. | Everyone |
| **Treatment Plan Library** | Ready-made treatment plan templates to apply to a client. | Director, Therapist |
| **Compliance Risk** | A monitor of client alerts that may need attention. | Director, Therapist |
| **Settings** | Practice settings (under a **"Reports"** heading). | Director only |

> Note: the menu item is named **"Calendar,"** and it opens the scheduling page.

To shrink the menu to just icons (handy on a smaller screen), click the small
button at the top of the menu; click it again to expand. To leave the app, use
**"Sign Out"** at the very bottom.

---

## The top bar

- **Search box ("Search clinical resources...").** Click it (or press
  **Ctrl + K**) to open a quick **command palette**. Start typing in the
  **"Type a command or search..."** box to jump to a page like **"Dashboard"** or
  **"Clients,"** or to pull up a client by name.
- **The "Schedule" button (with a + ).** This is your quick-create menu. Click it
  and you'll see:
  - **"Schedule Session"** — open the new-appointment window.
  - **"New Intake"** — start a new client file.
  - **"Create Note"** — open the note assistant *(Director and Therapist only;
    Jessica won't see this one).*
- **Your profile picture (top-right).** Click it for your account menu, including
  **"Settings"** (Director only) and **"Logout."**

A small trail of links (a "breadcrumb") appears near the top of most pages to
show where you are; click any part of it to step back.

---

## The "Clara" assistant (an early helper — please kick the tires)

In the bottom-right corner of every screen is a round button that opens
**Clara**, an AI assistant we're trying out. Click it (its label is
**"Open Clara AI Assistant"**), type a question in the **"Ask Clara about
today..."** box, and see what comes back. Close it with the Escape key or by
clicking the button again.

> 🧪 **Clara is brand-new and still learning.** Treat her as an experiment, not a
> finished tool — typing works best, and the voice feature may be rough. Please
> **only use made-up information** when trying her out, and tell us what felt
> helpful or off. Your reactions are exactly what we're looking for.

- **Analytics**
- **AI Documents**
