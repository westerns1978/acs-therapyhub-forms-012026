# Finding your way around

Once you sign in, the screen has three constant parts no matter where you go:

- a **menu down the left side** (your main navigation),
- a **bar across the top** (search, quick actions, Clara, and your profile), and
- a breadcrumb trail near the top of most pages that shows where you are.

> **On a phone or small screen,** the left menu is tucked away. Tap the **menu
> icon** (top-left) to slide it out. Everything below still applies — the app is
> built to work on a phone as well as a computer.

---

## The left-hand menu

This is how you move between the main areas of the app. Click any item to open
it. The item you're currently in is highlighted. You won't see *every* item
below — the menu only shows what your role uses (and a few areas are turned off
for the trial; see the bottom of this page).

| Menu item | What it's for | Who sees it during the trial |
|---|---|---|
| **Dashboard** | Your home screen and daily snapshot. | Everyone |
| **Clients** | Find a client and open their record. | Everyone |
| **Calendar** | The appointment schedule — view, add, and update sessions. | Everyone |
| **Forms** | The library of forms — assign them and review what comes back. | Everyone |
| **Treatment Plan Library** | Ready-made treatment plan templates to apply to a client. | Director, Therapist |
| **Compliance Risk** | A monitor of client alerts that may need attention. | Director, Therapist |
| **Financials** | Read-only billing reports — daily payments, outstanding balances, and revenue. | Director, Admin |

Below the main list, the Director also has a small **"Reports"** group:

| Reports item | What it's for | Who sees it |
|---|---|---|
| **Compliance Readiness** | A practice-wide view of what the compliance engine can verify today. | Director |
| **Settings** | Practice settings (Google, Zoom, and account details). | Director |

> The menu item is named **"Calendar,"** and it opens the scheduling page.

To shrink the menu to just icons (handy on a smaller screen), click the small
button at the top of the menu; click it again to expand. To leave the app, use
**"Sign Out"** at the very bottom.

---

## The top bar

- **Search box ("Search clinical resources...").** Click it (or press
  **Ctrl + K**) to open a quick **command palette**. Start typing to jump to a
  page like **"Dashboard"** or **"Clients,"** or to pull up a client by name. On
  a phone, the search appears as a small magnifying-glass icon.
- **The "Schedule" button (with a + ).** This is your quick-create menu. Click it
  and you'll see:
  - **"Schedule Session"** — open the new-appointment window.
  - **"New Intake"** — start a new client file.
  - **"Create Note"** — open the Smart Note Studio *(Director and Therapist only;
    the Office role won't see this one).*
- **Clara's button.** A small round picture of Clara, just left of the bell. See
  the next section.
- **The bell.** Notifications.
- **Your profile picture (far right).** Click it for your account menu, including
  **"Settings"** (Director only) and **"Logout."**

## The Clara assistant (an early helper — please kick the tires)

**Clara** is an AI assistant we're trying out. On staff screens she lives in the
**top bar**: look for the small round picture of Clara, just left of the bell,
labeled **"Open Clara clinical assistant."** Click it and a panel opens along the
right side of the screen. Type a question in the message box and see what comes
back. Close the panel with the Escape key or by clicking the button again.

Clara stays with you as you move around the app — open her on the Dashboard, walk
into a client's record, and your conversation is still there.

> **Clara is brand-new and still learning.** Treat her as an experiment, not a
> finished tool — typing works best, and the voice feature may be rough. Please
> **only use made-up information** when trying her out, and tell us what felt
> helpful or off. Your reactions are exactly what we're looking for.

- **Analytics**
- **AI Documents**
