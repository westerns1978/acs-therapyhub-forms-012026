# For the Therapist (Karen)

You have the **Therapist** role — the clinical view. You can work with clients,
the schedule, forms, treatment plans, and a client's billing. A few Director-only
areas (like **Settings**) and a few trial-disabled areas won't appear for you, and
that's expected.

## What's in your menu

- **Dashboard** — your daily snapshot.
- **Clients** — find and open any client record.
- **Calendar** — the appointment schedule.
- **Forms** — assign forms and review submissions.
- **Treatment Plan Library** — ready-made plan templates to apply to clients.
- **Compliance Risk** — a monitor of client alerts.

You won't see **Financials** or **Settings** — those are Director areas.

## Your dashboard

You land on the **Dashboard** after signing in. It opens with a short briefing —
a greeting and a sentence about today's sessions and whether any clients are
flagged. Below it you'll see **"Today's Schedule"** (click a session to open its
pre-session Green Room) and **"Clinical Guardrails,"** the top client compliance
flags from the engine, each with the Missouri rule it relates to.

## The client's record tabs

When you open a client, you'll see tabs across the top: **Overview, Documents,
Forms, Sessions, Assessment, Billing,** and **Treatment Plan.** The **Treatment
Plan** tab is for clinical roles like yours (the Office role doesn't see it). From
there you can view a client's active plan, **Edit** it, **Archive** it, or start a
**New plan**. See [Apply a treatment plan](06-common-tasks.md#apply-a-treatment-plan).

## Recording session hours

Marking a session **Completed** is how a client's program hours add up. When you
complete a session you'll choose a **session category** (counseling, education, or
group rehabilitative support), and those hours flow into the client's progress and
the compliance checks. See
[Update a session](06-common-tasks.md#update-a-session-complete-no-show-or-cancel).

## Early AI helpers — please try them and tell us

Two AI features are switched **on** for you during the trial. They're early
experiments — try them with **made-up information** and tell us how they felt.

- **Clara** — the assistant in the top bar (the small round picture, labeled
  **"Open Clara clinical assistant"**). Type a question to her. See
  [Finding your way around](02-navigation.md).
- **Smart Note Studio** — open it from the top **"Schedule"** menu →
  **"Create Note."** Draft a session note in your own words, optionally have AI
  format it into SOAP or DAP, then **"Save Note"** to add it to the client's record.

> **Sample data only.** These features send text to an AI service, so please don't
> enter real client information while testing them during the trial.

## Turned off during the trial

**Financials**, **Messages**, **Analytics**, and **AI Documents** are intentionally
hidden for now (see [Help & what's next](07-help-and-whats-next.md)).

> **Tell us what you think:** You'll spend the most time in the clinical screens —
> Clients, Treatment Plans, Sessions, and Forms. If a step feels clunky or a label
> is confusing, that's exactly what we want to hear.
