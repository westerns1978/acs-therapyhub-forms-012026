# For Office / Admin (Jessica)

You have the **Admin** role — the office view. It's built around the front-office
work: intake, scheduling, billing details, and forms. Clinical areas (like
treatment plans and the clinical alert monitor) are reserved for the Director and
Therapist, so you won't see those menus — that's by design, not a missing piece.

> 📝 A quick note on the word **"Admin."** Here it means the **office role**, not
> a super-user. It's the right fit for front-desk and intake work.

## What's in your menu

- **Dashboard** — your daily snapshot.
- **Clients** — find and open any client record.
- **Calendar** — the appointment schedule.
- **Messages** — the communication center.
- **Forms** — assign forms and review what clients send back.
- **Financials** — read-only billing reports (daily payments, outstanding
  balances, and revenue). New for the day-30 review; shared with the Director.

You won't see **Treatment Plan Library**, **Compliance Risk**, or **Settings** —
those are clinical/Director areas.

## What you can do

- [Add a client](06-common-tasks.md#add-a-client) — the new-client intake wizard.
- [Open a client's record](06-common-tasks.md#open-a-clients-record) and update
  their contact and billing details.
- [View the schedule](06-common-tasks.md#view-the-schedule),
  [schedule a session](06-common-tasks.md#schedule-a-session), and
  [cancel a session](06-common-tasks.md#cancel-a-session).
- [Assign a form](06-common-tasks.md#assign-a-form) to clients and
  [review submissions](06-common-tasks.md#review-form-submissions).

## What you'll see on a client's record

When you open a client, you'll see the tabs **Overview, Documents, Forms,** and
**Sessions.** (The **Treatment Plan** tab is clinical, so it won't appear for
you.)

If you open **"Edit Client,"** you can update the **Contact** section (name,
phone, email, date of birth) and the **"Billing & Legal"** section (case number,
county, probation officer, payment source). The **"Clinical"** section is locked
for the office role — you'll see a small **"Therapist / Director only"** label
there. That's expected.

## Your dashboard

You land on the **Dashboard** after signing in. You'll see the three top figures
(**"Compliance Rate," "Monthly Revenue," "Active Clients"**) and **"Today's
Schedule."** There's also a **"Risk Monitor"** summary — opening the *full* Risk
Monitor is a clinical-staff area, so those particular buttons may simply return
you to the Dashboard. No harm done.

## The "Clara" assistant — feel free to try it

The round **Clara** button in the bottom-right corner is available to you. Type a
question to it and see what comes back. It's an early experiment we'd love your
reaction to.

> ⚠️ **Sample data only.** Clara sends text to an AI service, so please use only
> made-up information when trying it during the trial.

*(The "Create Note" / Smart Note Studio assistant is a clinical tool, so it isn't
part of your menu.)*

## Turned off during the trial

**Analytics** and **AI Documents** are intentionally hidden for
now (see [Help & what's next](07-help-and-whats-next.md)).

> 💬 **Tell us what you think:** You'll be in **Clients, Calendar,** and **Forms**
> the most. If adding a client or scheduling a session takes more clicks than it
> should, tell us — smoothing out the office workflow is a top priority for the
> trial.
